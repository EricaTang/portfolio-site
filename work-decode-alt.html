Decode

# print & screen menu # indesign # figma

For this project, I created both a print menu and a screen menu for the fragrance brand that I ideated, called "Decode".


Decode emphasizes the individuality of scents. We hope that our customers can, through using our products, open their sensory languages, and decode their own scent. Decode products range from scents for space to scents for the body. Our offerings are divided into four main categories: mood-scented candles, four season aromas, Eau de parfum, and body and hand lotions.
