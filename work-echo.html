Echo

# logo # illustrator

For this logo project, I am creating a logo for the brand I ideated - "echo." "Echo" means reflecting, reminding, resonating, and repeating.


It is a software that combines music-playing, social networking, and journal-keeping. Users can receive daily music recommendations, join music-based communities, trace their own music taste, and record their personal stories that are related to a particular song or genre.


Echo is oriented to music lovers and people who are interested in building connections with individuals or groups of people that share similar music tastes.
